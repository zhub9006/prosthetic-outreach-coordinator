# Cross-Region Meeting Points

This document identifies the optimal meeting location for the three outreach coordinators in Chicago, Boston, and Atlanta.

---

## 🏆 Recommended Meeting Venue: COSI (Center of Science and Industry)

| Field | Details |
|-------|---------|
| **Venue** | COSI — Center of Science and Industry |
| **Address** | 333 West Broad Street, Columbus, OH 43215 |
| **Coordinates** | 39.9597, -83.0067 |
| **Website** | [cosi.org](https://cosi.org) |
| **Importance Rating** | 0.344 (highly significant landmark) |

### Why COSI?
- ✅ **Geographically central** — equidistant-ish between all three cities
- ✅ **Event-friendly venue** — science museums often have meeting/event spaces
- ✅ **Downtown Columbus** — excellent accessibility via I-71 and I-70
- ✅ **Nearby amenities** — restaurants, hotels, parking
- ✅ **Neutral ground** — not associated with any single region

- 150+ vendors, diverse cuisines, perfect for group coordination
