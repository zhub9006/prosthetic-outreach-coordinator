# Prosthetic Outreach Coordinator

Volunteer task coordination for our prosthetic outreach initiative — tracking teams, tasks, and progress across regions.

## 🎯 Mission

Coordinate volunteer efforts to improve access to affordable prosthetic solutions through research, outreach, and regional coordination.

## 📋 Quick Start

1. Browse the [Issues](https://github.com/zhub9006/prosthetic-outreach-coordinator/issues) tab to find tasks to claim.
2. Use labels to filter by region or task type:
   - `region:chicago` — Chicago outreach team
   - `region:boston` — Boston outreach team
   - `region:atlanta` — Atlanta outreach team
   - `type:research` — Research & data gathering
   - `type:logistics` — Logistics & supply chain
   - `type:community` — Community outreach & engagement
   - `type:clinical` — Clinical trial coordination
3. Comment on an issue to claim it.
4. Push your work and link it back to the issue.

### Key Trials for Our Initiative

1. **ReMotion Prosthetic Knee** — NCT04700085
   - $80 affordable mechanical prosthetic knee (vs. standard $1,000–$5,000)
   - Completed | Sint Maartenskliniek
   - Reverse innovation: proven affordable mobility solution

2. **3D Printing Overdentures** — NCT06155630
   - Digital workflow for faster, cheaper prosthesis fabrication
   - McGill University | Not Yet Recruiting
   - Cost comparison being studied

3. **NMES Home Rehabilitation** — NCT06830876
   - Cost-effective home-based muscle rehabilitation
   - O&P Clinical Innovations | Currently Recruiting
   - Portable device, no clinic visits needed
