# Prosthetic Outreach Coordinator

Volunteer task coordination for our prosthetic outreach initiative — tracking teams, tasks, and progress across regions.

## 🎯 Mission

Coordinate volunteer efforts to improve access to affordable prosthetic solutions.

## 📋 Quick Start

1. Browse the [Issues](https://github.com/zhub9006/prosthetic-outreach-coordinator/issues) tab to find tasks to claim.
2. Use labels to filter by region or task type:
   - `region:chicago` — Chicago outreach team
   - `region:boston` — Boston outreach team
   - `region:atlanta` — Atlanta outreach team
   - `type:research` — Research & data gathering
   - `type:logistics` — Logistics & supply chain
   - `type:community` — Community outreach & engagement
   - `type:clinical` — Clinical trial coordination
3. Comment on an issue to claim it.
4. Push your work and link it back to the issue.

## 🏥 Clinical Trial Reference

Key study identified: **NCT04700085** — ReMotion Prosthetic Knee (Reverse Innovation)
- Sponsor: Sint Maartenskliniek
- Status: Completed (2019)
- Focus: $80 affordable mechanical prosthetic knee vs. standard $1,000–$5,000 knees
- Conditions: Lower limb amputation (above-knee)
- Key finding: ReMotion Knee provides basic functional mobility at a fraction of the cost
